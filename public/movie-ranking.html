The Best Movies According to Parvez

My top movies of all-time.

Jersey

This movie is about a cricket player who quits his profession due to health issues and joins a job but later decides to resume his cricket journey after a decade gap due to some forsen situations and faces criticism from society and even with family for this return to cricket.

Super 30

This movie is about a teacher in Bihar, India who provides free tutuion for Below Poverty Line students and help them to clear entrance exam. In this journey he faces several financial problems.

Taare Zameen Par

This movie is about a child who is different from other child in terms of learning and expressing feeling. But soon he becomes extrovert after meeting a teacher.

Jai Bhim

This movie is about reality of life of lower caste people in India where Upper Caste people tries to overpower them.

Swades

A NRI who returns to India to see his grownup place and meet his mother and eventually situations and problems in India make him to stay back.
